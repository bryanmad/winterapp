Winter Rules


From Oct 1st to Oct 31st inclusive

1. Preferred lies on the fairway and fringes of greens (ie. the general area cut to fairway length) . Mark your ball, lift, clean and place within 6 inches, no nearer the hole.

2. In the semi rough, mark your ball, lift, clean and replace.

3. In the rough, play the ball as it lies.

4. Relief from casual water in the General Area is by taking the nearest point of relief no nearer the hole, and dropping the ball within 1 club length of that point, the ball may be cleaned.

November 1st to 31st March 2024
As above with these additions

1. The Seniors Society will be keeping all par 5's as par 5's (as per the summer card).

2. Bounce boards, mats and tees may be used on fairways and fringes of greens. These are not to be used in semi rough or rough areas of the course.

3. Semi rough will be lift, clean and replace.

4. Rough will be play as it lies.

5. Drivers are not to be used on bounce boards, mats or tees after the initial tee shot, they can, however, be used from the ground.

6. Bunkers will be lift, rake and place, unless otherwise stated on the whiteboard at registration.
