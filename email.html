2024 Winter app

All,

As most of you know, we have a Winter "app" for use when conditions don't allow scores to be entered through BRS. Your WHS handicap remains as your only legitimate golf handicap, but the "app" provides a method of competing over winter, from October to March.
link to Winter App

Using your phone, once you open the "app", it's probably best to save a shortcut to your home screen or bookmark the page. If you are in need of any further assistance, now, or in the future, please do not hesitate to ask, or get in touch by email, using the link below.

Notes...

 

The 2024 Winter app remains much the same as last year with some small changes to help readability and 2 minor additions:

 

1 - There is now an additional menu item to allow scoring for your fourball (or part of your fourball) as an alternative to individual scoring.

 

2 - During individual score input, tapping on the "hole 1" etc. will enter your "par equivalent" strokes for the hole. You can then change the strokes using + and - as before. This is optional and your score can still be entered the same way as before, using only the + and -.

 

Apart from obvious cosmetic differences, any other changes are operational and would not be noticed in normal use.
